Hi, I'm Arpit Mishra

I'm a fine art photographer specializing in street and landscape photography. My journey with photography began as a way to escape the daily routine and see the world through a different lens. What started as a personal exploration has evolved into a passion for capturing moments that tell stories and evoke emotions.

My street photography focuses on the authentic, unscripted moments of urban life ‚Äî the quiet poetry found in everyday scenes that most people walk past without noticing. In my landscape work, I seek to capture the raw, untamed beauty of nature, from dramatic mountain vistas to serene coastal horizons.

Each photograph in my collection is carefully curated and available as a limited edition fine art print. Whether you're an art collector, interior designer, or photography enthusiast, I hope my work brings beauty, inspiration, and a unique perspective to your space.
